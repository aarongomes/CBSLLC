SOW - Statement Of Work
For smooth and efficient project management in any enterprise, a well-drafted Statement of Work is crucial. An SOW is a documentary that outlines the activities specific to any project and the deliverables in the project along with the set timeline. This document also ensures that the businesses across various industries are taking the essential steps for aligning their employees, products, services, and practices with the evolving technology and trends. Certitude Business Solutions LLC provides the companies with a well-drafted solution for developing as well as managing the Statement of Work.

With our Statement of Work management solutions, we outline the best course of action that any company can take for the smooth functioning of different processes. Be it a high-level consultation for a yearlong project or independent contractors for the extension of any contract, Certitude Business is always ready with their SOW solutions. We take the tension and pain out of any contract business to ensure a smooth flow. Our solutions enable the hiring managers to find right partners and align them with the business developments. We do all of this while ensuring a streamlined flow of operations.

We help in mitigating risks and maximizing the returns by defining the project scope, deliverables, objectives, and timelines, as per the contract or SOW. Apart from that, the Statement of Work that we draft offers a visibility and consistency with the projects, timelines, cost, milestones achieved etc. Companies in various industries like accounting, information technology, supply chain, and procurement can contact us to get our excellent Statement of Work management services.
