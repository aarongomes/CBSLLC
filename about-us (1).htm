Sustainability
Sustainability is an integral part of our company. It is our obligation to put the best interests of our employees, customers, communities and the environment at the forefront of CBSLLC’s strategies and goals. We are committed to providing the best service and upholding our values and ethical standards in all of our company activities.

Our company’s Sustainability initiative includes four pillars:

People
We connect talented individuals with the world’s most successful organizations every day to grow the global workforce.

Community
We believe in giving back to the communities where we live in. We believe community involvement is an important piece of personal development and gives individuals the opportunity to foster relationships within their community.

Environment
Recognizing that our work has an indirect effect on the environment, we are committed to reducing our carbon impact, while educating and empowering our employees to make more environmentally responsible choices.

Governance and Ethics
We conduct business according to the highest ethical standards, and based on our shared values. Our employees abide by a code of conduct that helps our employees make sound decisions in complex situations involving our company and our business.

Diversity

CBSLLC is a Women-Owned/Minority Owned Organization. To its founder and the team at CBSLLC, Diversity is just not a business aspect; in today’s business world it is the most vital feature to create an increasingly global, multi-ethnic and competitive environment. Our commitment to diversity and inclusion is reflected in our workforce. CBSLLC supports our clientele’s diversity goals by recruiting the best qualified candidates regardless of their gender, race, religious beliefs, sexual orientation and special needs. We also partner with groups that advocate for people with disabilities, people of color and Military/Veterans.
